About

The APSIPA Vietnam 3MT Competition 2025 aims to foster academic communication skills by encouraging graduate students and early-career researchers to present their work in a concise and engaging manner.

  • Format: 3-minute oral presentation supported by a single slide.
  • Audience: Graduate students, young researchers, and academics.
  • Objective: Showcase research, enhance communication, and connect with the APSIPA community.
